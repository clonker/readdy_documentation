void readdy::kernel::scpu::observables::SCPUReactionCounts::evaluate ( )
inlineoverridevirtual

Will be called automagically when shouldExecuteCallback() is true. Can also be called manually and will then place the observable's results into the result member (in case of a readdy::model::Observable), based on the current state of the system.
